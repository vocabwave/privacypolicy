Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 18 June 2024

Introduction

Welcome to VocabWave, a platform dedicated to enhancing your vocabulary skills through both our website and Android app. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our website and Android app.

Information Collection and Use

No Server-Side Data Collection

At VocabWave, we do not collect or store any user data on our servers. All interactions with our website and Android app are handled directly on your device, and no personal information is transmitted to or stored on our servers. This includes:

• No User Accounts: We do not require you to create an account or log in to use our services.
• No Server Logs: We do not maintain any server logs that could store your IP address, browser details, or other metadata.

Local Storage (Website)

Any data that is generated or stored while using the VocabWave website is saved directly to your browser's local storage. This includes, but is not limited to:

• Vocabulary Lists: Any vocabulary words you save or lists you create are stored locally on your device.
• User Preferences: Any settings or preferences you customize are saved in your browser’s local storage.

Local Storage (Android App)

Any data generated or stored while using the VocabWave Android app is saved locally on your device. This includes, but is not limited to:

• Vocabulary Lists: Any vocabulary words you save or lists you create are stored locally on your device.
• User Preferences: Any settings or preferences you customize are saved in the app's local storage.

User Control

You have full control over the data stored in your browser’s local storage and on your Android device. You can manage or delete this data at any time through your browser settings or device settings. Here’s how you can manage your data:

• Clear Local Storage (Website): You can clear your browser’s local storage through the settings menu in your browser. This will remove all data related to VocabWave.
• Clear App Data (Android App): You can clear the app’s data through your device’s application settings. This will remove all data related to VocabWave.
• Uninstall App (Android App): If you prefer to remove all data, you can uninstall the app from your device, which will delete all associated data.

Third-Party Services

VocabWave does not use third-party services that collect or store user data. We do not integrate with any external analytics providers, advertising networks, or social media platforms. Your interactions with VocabWave remain private and confined to your own device.

Cookies

We do not use cookies on VocabWave. All necessary data is managed through local storage, ensuring that no tracking information is sent to external servers.

Permissions (Android App)

The VocabWave Android app may request certain permissions to function correctly. However, these permissions are strictly for app functionality and not for data collection. For example:

• Storage Access: To save your vocabulary lists and preferences.
• Network Access: To search online about any word.

We do not use these permissions to collect or transmit any personal data.

Data Security

Although we do not collect or store any personal data, we are committed to ensuring that your information remains secure within your browser and on your device. We recommend the following practices to enhance your data security:

• Regular Updates: Ensure your browser and Android app are up to date to protect against security vulnerabilities.
• Secure Browsing: Use secure and private browsing modes if you are concerned about data privacy.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, along with the updated effective date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your data.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or your privacy while using VocabWave, please contact us at contactvocabwave@gmail.com.

At VocabWave, your privacy is our priority. By ensuring that no user data is collected or stored on our servers and giving you full control over your data through your browser and Android device, we aim to provide a secure and private learning experience. Thank you for trusting VocabWave to help you enhance your vocabulary skills.
